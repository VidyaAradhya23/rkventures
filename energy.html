Electric Vehicle Packs

High Performance EV Batteries

We distribute advanced EV battery modules designed for Indian road conditions. Our EV cells offer rapid charging capability, safety against mechanical impact, and stable discharge rates under peak operational heat loads.

01

AIS-156 Phase 2 Certified

Fully meets the stringent safety mandates defined by the Government of India, including thermal fuse links and pressure release valves.

02

Smart Active BMS

Constantly balances individual cells, optimizes thermal profiles, and transmits State-of-Charge (SOC) data to vehicle dash units.

03

High Lifecycle Life

Maintains over 80% battery capacity even after 2,500 full charge-discharge cycles, providing years of driving range stability.
