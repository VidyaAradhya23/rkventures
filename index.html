Support & Info

Frequently Asked Questions

Common answers regarding our business divisions, servicing, and order workflows.

Is RK Ventures a direct manufacturer of PURE Power battery systems? +

No, RK Ventures is an authorized distributor and solutions provider for official PURE Power Energies products. We manage local inventory distribution, custom setup planning, technical deployment, and post-sales maintenance guarantees.

What areas do you cover for Construction and Interior Design? +

We provide full turnkey construction, structural design, and modular interior fittings across residential housing projects, commercial corporate offices, and premium renovations. We service key regional hubs with structured crews.

What are the safety standards of the batteries you distribute? +

All PURE Power battery packs and energy storage modules are fully AIS-156 certified. They feature advanced active Battery Management Systems (BMS) with over-charge, thermal runaway protection, and nano-PCM cooling coatings.

Can I customize construction layouts or modular designs? +

Yes. Our Construction & Interior Works division operates on a bespoke consult-and-build model. We generate detailed 3D models and blueprints for client approvals before commencing execution work.
