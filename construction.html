Execution Path

Our Standard Working Process

We follow a strict step-by-step project lifecycle to complete works on time and within agreed limits.

01

Consult & Survey

We meet on-site to review land topography, capture precise dimensions, and document core styling expectations.

02

3D Visualizations

Our architects construct detailed 3D models and layout floorplans so you see structural shapes before groundwork begins.

03

Cost Estimation

We provide an itemized Bill of Quantities (BOQ), matching materials, paint types, and timber selections to your budget limits.

04

Turnkey Construction

Our civil crews, carpenters, and finishing technicians execute construction on-site under active engineering oversight.

05

Handover & Check

After full deep cleaning, we conduct final alignment checks and hand over keys along with maintenance manuals.
